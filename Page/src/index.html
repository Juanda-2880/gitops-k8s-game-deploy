About The Game

Overwatch 2 is a free-to-play, always-on, and ever-evolving live game. Team up with friends and dive into the reimagined PvP experience as a time-traveling freedom fighter, a beat-dropping battlefield DJ, or one of over 30 other unique heroes as you battle it out around the globe.

With dynamic new maps, updates to classic heroes, and a shift to a tactical 5v5 format, the game emphasizes individual impact while keeping teamwork essential.
